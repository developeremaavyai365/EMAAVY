Stage 01 of 5 — Call Connects on the EMAAVY voice intelligence pipeline.

Stage overview

What happens during Call Connects and why it matters.

Ring is the ingest stage. Whether inbound support, outbound campaign, or supervisor-monitored sales, EMAAVY attaches to the audio stream at connect and enriches it with caller ID, campaign tags, and consent state before transcription even starts.

What happens on the call

Signals and events your team sees during this stage.

Audio capture

Stream begins at answer — inbound or outbound.

Session metadata

Caller ID, dialed number, and campaign ID logged.

Compliance flags

Recording consent and regional rules applied.

Speaker context

Diarization initialized for downstream stages.
