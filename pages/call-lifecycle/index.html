How the five-stage pipeline works

EMAAVY treats every call as a continuous intelligence loop — not a recording you analyze tomorrow. Audio enters at ring, becomes text in milliseconds, gets scored live, triggers your stack, and feeds the next program automatically.

Ring → TranscribeZero-gap capture and streaming STT on the wire.
Analyze liveSentiment, intent, and compliance per turn.
Act instantlyCRM, WhatsApp, and webhooks from real speech.
Learn & improveScripts and routing refine every campaign.
