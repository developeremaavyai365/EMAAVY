03 Call lifecycle · Score Live

Stage overview

The analyze stage is EMAAVY's reasoning layer. Your configured LLM stack interprets meaning, emotion, and risk on each turn, feeding the intelligence matrix dashboards and trigger engine simultaneously.

What happens on the call

Signals and events your team sees during this stage.

  • Sentiment timeline shifts green, amber, or red as tone changes
  • Intent classified as buy, explore, objection, or reject per turn
  • Compliance lexicon flags risky phrases for regulated industries
  • Objection tags feed script coaching and funnel analytics

How EMAAVY powers this stage

  1. 01Reason

    Route GPT, Claude, Gemini, Qwen, or Grok per workflow.

  2. 02Score

    Apply sentiment, intent, and compliance models in parallel.

  3. 03Alert

    Notify supervisors when thresholds breach.

  4. 04Summarize

    Rolling call summary for handoff and CRM notes.

Business outcomes

  • Decisions based on live conversation state, not post-call guesswork
  • Compliance teams catch issues during the call
  • Sales leaders see intent shift before deals slip away
