05 Call lifecycle · Models Improve

Stage overview

Learn turns operational data into compounding advantage. EMAAVY aggregates outcomes across campaigns to recommend script changes, STT/LLM routing tweaks, and agent coaching focus areas — without a separate data science team.

What happens on the call

Signals and events your team sees during this stage.

  • Script variants ranked by conversion and handle time
  • Model routing adjusted for language and intent segments
  • Coaching patterns surfaced for under-performing agents
  • Funnel trends exported for executive and ops reviews

How EMAAVY powers this stage

  1. 01Aggregate

    Roll up scores, outcomes, and durations across campaigns.

  2. 02Recommend

    Suggest script and routing changes from win/loss patterns.

  3. 03Loop

    Feed results into agent builder and campaign defaults.

  4. 04Report

    Weekly intelligence summaries for leadership.

Business outcomes

  • Conversion rates improve quarter over quarter without new headcount
  • Cost per outcome drops as routing optimizes automatically
  • Coaching becomes data-led instead of anecdotal
