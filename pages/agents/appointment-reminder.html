Overview

What the Contextual Appointment Reminder does and when to deploy it on EMAAVY.

A reminder agent that confirms upcoming appointments, offers reschedule options when callers are running late, and snoozes follow-ups — reducing no-shows without sounding robotic.

Context-aware branching detects lateness, cancellations, and confirmation intent. Calendar integrations update slots live while supervisors see reminder outcomes in the campaign dashboard.
