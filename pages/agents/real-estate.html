Overview

What the Real Estate Lead Qualification does and when to deploy it on EMAAVY.

An outbound voice agent that qualifies property inquiries, captures budget and timeline, handles objections, and books site visits — turning cold leads into showroom appointments without human dialers.

Pre-built prompts cover new launches, resale, and rental programs. The agent scores intent in real time, routes hot leads to sales reps, and syncs disposition data to your CRM after every call.
