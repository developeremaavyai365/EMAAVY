Overview

What the Intelligent Support Playbook does and when to deploy it on EMAAVY.

An inbound support agent with intent-specific playbooks — login issues, billing questions, product interest, and complaints each get tailored flows with smart escalation to humans.

Intent classification routes callers to the right playbook in seconds. Resolution steps pull from your knowledge base while supervisors monitor CSAT risk on live dashboards.
