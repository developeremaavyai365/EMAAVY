Overview

What the Appointment Booking does and when to deploy it on EMAAVY.

An inbound scheduling agent that checks availability, books slots, sends confirmations, and handles reschedule or cancellation requests — 24/7 without front-desk bottlenecks.

Connects to Cal.com or Google Calendar for live availability. Callers receive SMS or WhatsApp confirmations while your team sees every booking logged against the right contact record.
