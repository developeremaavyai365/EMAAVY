Privacy Policy

How EMAAVY collects, processes, and protects call audio, transcripts, and account data.

01 Data we process

Call and account data

We process call audio, transcripts, metadata, and tool execution logs to deliver voice agent services. Customers control retention windows per sub-account — default 90 days for recordings, configurable to zero-storage modes.

Vector embeddings derived from your knowledgebases remain in your chosen region. EMAAVY does not train foundation models on customer call content without explicit opt-in.

02 Your rights

Access & deletion

Submit data access or deletion requests to privacy@emaavy.com. Enterprise customers receive DPA and SCC packages with designated security contacts.
