01 The challenge

What Warehouse by Mudita needed to solve

The marketing team had a list of 5,000 high-value prospects but email campaigns plateaued at a 12% registration rate. Manual calling was too slow for the exhibition timeline, and scripts in English alone missed a large Hindi-speaking audience.

02 EMAAVY solution

How EMAAVY delivered

EMAAVY deployed Priya, a campaign voice agent fluent in Hindi and English with natural code-switching. Every call captured intent, objections, and registration details live — then triggered WhatsApp confirmations automatically.
