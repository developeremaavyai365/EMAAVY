BPO · Quality assurance

Overview

The challenge NextCall BPO faced before deploying EMAAVY.

Quality teams could only audit about 5% of calls manually. Coaching was delayed, compliance gaps were discovered days later, and supervisors had no live signal when calls went off-script.

Scaling headcount to review every conversation was economically impossible across their regulated client programs.

Key capabilities

EMAAVY ingested the full call stream, transcribed in real time, and scored every turn for sentiment, compliance keywords, and buyer intent. Supervisors received coaching alerts during active calls instead of after batch reviews.
