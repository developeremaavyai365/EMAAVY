Overview

Latency is a product feature, not an afterthought. Engineering and ops share one pane for component-level timing, historical trends, and automated alerts when inference or media streams exceed thresholds.

What you see in the dashboard

Key views and signals available in this module.

  • P50 / P95 / P99 latency charts per stack component
  • Per-component breakdown: STT, LLM, TTS, webhook execution
  • SLA breach alerting to Slack, email, or PagerDuty
  • Historical trend analysis for capacity upgrades

How it works on EMAAVY

  1. 01Timestamp

    Each pipeline stage records millisecond markers.

  2. 02Aggregate

    Histograms compute percentiles per region and provider.

  3. 03Compare

    Routes across STT/LLM providers highlight slow paths.

  4. 04Alert

    Policies fire when P99 crosses configured limits.

  5. 05Report

    Weekly infra summaries for platform and customer success teams.
