Overview

Stop guessing from dispositions typed after the fact. Intent Mapping classifies every turn into buyer signals — high intent, exploring, objection, not interested — and visualizes distribution in real time.

What you see in the dashboard

Key views and signals available in this module.

  • Intent funnel visualization with live stage counts
  • Trend-over-time chart for campaign A/B comparisons
  • Filters by agent, language, product line, or region
  • Exportable snapshots for weekly business reviews

How it works on EMAAVY

  1. 01Transcribe

    Live text feeds the intent classifier each turn.

  2. 02Classify

    LLM + rules score buy / hold / reject / objection types.

  3. 03Aggregate

    Funnel buckets update without batch jobs.

  4. 04Trigger

    Threshold crossings can fire webhooks or CRM updates.

  5. 05Learn

    Misclassifications feed back into model tuning.

Typical use cases

  • Prioritizing callbacks for high-intent leads
  • Spotting script fatigue when objection rates spike
  • Aligning marketing messaging with real buyer language
